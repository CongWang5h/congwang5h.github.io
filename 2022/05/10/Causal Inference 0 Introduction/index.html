Convex combination

The reason that

A<BA < B A<B

is that large 1−q1-q1−q pull the value of AAA towards (3)(3)(3), which is much smaller than (1)(1)(1), resulting in a small value of AAA.

Statement A
1−q=P(X=0∣Z=1)1-q =P(X=0| Z=1)1−q=P(X=0∣Z=1) is large   ⟹  \implies⟹ Drug users are more likely to be women.

Statement B
(3)<(1)(3) < (1)(3)<(1)   ⟹  \implies⟹ Women drug users are less likely to recover than men drug users .

If we randomly select a drug user in the population, it is more likely to be woman who is more difficult to get recovered; thus it appears this person is more difficult to recover even with treatment.
